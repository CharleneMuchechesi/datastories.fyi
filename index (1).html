About Me

Who I Am

📸 Your photo here

I'm a data analyst based in Harare, Zimbabwe, currently working at Axcentium (formerly Deloitte Zimbabwe). My day-to-day spans audit analytics — GL completeness testing, fraud detection per ISA standards, revenue recomputations, and anomaly detection across banking sector clients using SQL, Excel, and IDEA.

But what drives me is the bigger picture. I want to use data to address real African development problems — financial inclusion for unbanked communities, education equity, and rural economic development. The questions Zimbabwe and the continent face deserve rigorous data thinking, not just spreadsheets.

I hold a BSc Honours in Informatics (2.1) from NUST and I'm actively building toward a data science specialisation — working through the Google Data Analytics Certificate, Andrew Ng's Deep Learning Specialization, and a Power BI (PL-300) certification.

Currently building: a public data portfolio at datastories.fyi — projects using real, open datasets to surface insights on education equity, financial access, and development economics across Southern Africa.
